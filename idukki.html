IDUKKI

Idukki is one of Kerala's most scenic tourist destinations, known for its mist-covered hills, lush forests, wildlife sanctuaries, waterfalls, and tea plantations. Popular attractions include Munnar, Idukki Arch Dam, Eravikulam National Park, and Periyar Wildlife Sanctuary. The district offers opportunities for trekking, wildlife viewing, boating, and nature photography, making it a favorite destination for nature lovers and adventure seekers.
