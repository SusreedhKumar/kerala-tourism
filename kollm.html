Kollam Tourism Kollam is one of the oldest port cities in Kerala, located on the banks of the beautiful Ashtamudi Lake. Known for its rich history, natural beauty, and backwater tourism, Kollam attracts visitors from across the world. Major tourist attractions include the scenic Ashtamudi Lake, Jatayu Earth's Center, Thangassery Lighthouse, Palaruvi Waterfalls, and Munroe Island. Visitors can enjoy houseboat cruises, adventure activities, and explore the region's cultural heritage. With its combination of beaches, lakes, waterfalls, and historical sites, Kollam is a popular destination for nature lovers and tourists seeking a peaceful travel experience.

Main Tourist places in kollam

1. Ashtamudi Lake – Famous backwater destination known for houseboat cruises and scenic beauty. 2. Jatayu Earth's Center – Home to one of the world's largest bird sculptures and offers adventure activities. 3. Thenmala Ecotourism – India's first planned ecotourism destination with nature trails and adventure zones. 4. Palaruvi Waterfalls – A beautiful waterfall surrounded by lush forests. 5. Thangassery Lighthouse – Historic lighthouse offering panoramic views of the Arabian Sea. 6. Kollam Beach – Popular beach for sunsets and relaxation. 7. Munroe Island – Known for serene backwaters, canals, and village life experiences. 8. Sasthamkotta Lake – The largest freshwater lake in Kerala.
