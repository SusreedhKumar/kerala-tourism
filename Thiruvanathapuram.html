Trivandrum

Thiruvananthapuram and Tourism Thiruvananthapuram, also known as Trivandrum, is the capital city of Kerala. It is famous for its rich cultural heritage, beautiful beaches, historic temples, and lush greenery. The city is home to the renowned Sree Padmanabhaswamy Temple, one of India's most important temples. Popular tourist attractions include Kovalam Beach, Napier Museum, and Poovar Island. With its blend of natural beauty, historical landmarks, and vibrant culture, Thiruvananthapuram is a major tourism destination in southern India.

Main tourist places

Sree Padmanabhaswamy Temple, Kovalam Beach, Veli Tourist Village, Shankumugham Beach, Neyyar Dam, Napier Museum, Thiruvananthapuram Zoo, Ponmudi.
